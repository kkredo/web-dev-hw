Why Did I Choose This Project?

I decided to make an instructable on how to cast a fly rod because that was something new to me when I moved to Missoula my freshmen year. I had to watch numerous Youtube videos and read a handful of articles until I understood partly what I was doing. Of course, countless hours on the water is the best teacher, but I thought this subject would be interesting to do.


What Does it Mean for You?

Besides being an interesting subject to me, fishing is a passion of mine. I grew up fishing in the local harbors and lakes back home in Southern California and althogh I did not fly fish then, the same principle applied. Spending quality time with my dad on the water and the endless puzzle of trying to figure out what the the fish are doing and how they are behaving in an effort to catch one. The endless scouring of details of current, air temp, water temp, tidal movement, lunar phases, spawning time, all were studied and recorded until eventually you become highly knowledgeable about the fishery. It is a time consuming challenge, but highly rewarding and the fact that it is constantly changing keeps the acquistion of knowledge never ending. I believe that being able to share one's passion with another is essential to connection and teaching is part of that. Being able to introduce someone to your world and teach them about it and involve them is an unique experience we all ought to do.
